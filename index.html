Delete Account – Quiz Royale

You can delete your account directly from within the Quiz Royale app.

Where to find the delete option

To delete your account, go to your Profile page in the app.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the Quiz Royale app
  2. Go to the Profile section
  3. Tap on "Delete Account" at the bottom of the page
  4. Confirm the deletion

What will be deleted

  • Your username and ranking
  • Your game history and statistics
  • Your friend list

What is retained

  • Anonymous technical logs (not linked to your profile) for app analytics – kept for 30 days max

Account deletion is immediate and irreversible.
